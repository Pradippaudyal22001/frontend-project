Famous Food

  1. →　 Okinawa Soba

    Okinawa's soul food "Okinawa soba" uses flour instead of buckwheat flour for noodles,
    and soup varies depending on the shop, such as pork bone base and bonito base.
    The toppings are three pieces of pork, kamaboko, and green onions, but it is recommended to
    compare them because they vary depending on the region and shops♪


  2. → Tacos & Taco Rice

    "Taco Rice" a typical dish of Mexico "Tacos" Okinawan food with ingredients on rice.
    Born in Kin Town, where the U.S. Military Base is located, it was originally designed for U.S. soldiers.
    It goes well with salsa sauce and rice, and it is popular as a B-class gourmet representing
    Okinawa with perfect volume.


  3. → Pork Egg Onigiri

    Using pork cans, ingredients familiar and excellent to okinawans "Pork Egg Onigiri"
    Is Okinawa's soul food that is lined up at bento shops and convenience stores. It is said that
    the origin of the classic Hawaiian gourmet "Spam Onigiri" was transmitted to Okinawa by immigrants.
    Fluffy omelets and spam sandwiched between hot rice are a combination that becomes addictive once eaten.


  4. → Agu Pig (Island Pig)

    Pork is the main dish of Okinawan cuisine. Pork has long been said to be delicious
    except for the cry, so it is a popular ingredient in Okinawa.
    Among them, "Agu pig (island pig)" is especially popular inside and outside the prefecture.
    It is said that the meat, which is high-quality, soft, and has a taste of sweetness, has supported
    Okinawa's food culture since the Ryukyu Dynasty.

  5. →　Goya Champloo

    "Goya Champloo", a popular gourmet using typical island vegetables and bitter gourd!
    Champloo means "chamuse" in the Okinawan dialect, but it is also used for stir-frying vegetables and tofu.
    In addition to gourds, it is common to put island tofu, pork chong meat (pork), and eggs.

  6. → Okinawa Tempura

    "Okinawa Tempura" is characterized by moist thick batter.Usually, only flour and eggs are used for
    tempura batter, but okinawa tempura batter is recommended to be seasoned with dashi and salt.
    Therefore, it can be delicious without attaching anything.
    The main ingredients used for tempura are white fish, squid, mozuku, etc. It is sold not only at
    tempura shops but also in convenience stores recently.
